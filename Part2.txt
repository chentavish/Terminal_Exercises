1. What does the man command do? Type in man rm. How do you scroll and get out?

The man command will list out the manual for the command. 
Control D will jump half a page  
Control B will go back a page
Control U will go back half a page

2. Look at the man page for ls. What does the -l flag do? What does the -a flag do?

-l flag will list the files in a list format
-a will list all files and folders

alt + arrow keys will jump between words

5. How do you move your cursor to the beginning in terminal?

pressing the alt + a will get to the end of a line

6. How do you delete a word (without pressing backspace multiple times) in terminal?

option + delete  will delete the word the cursor is on currently

Shell is what is actually handling the commands

9. What is an relative path?
